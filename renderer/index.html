Lords of Twilight

— a tale of the third age of midnight —
Long after Doomdark fell and the Ice Crown was shattered, a wound tore open in the deep east: the Abyssal Rift — a pit of oil-dark fire crowned by a crystal spire, pouring living shadow into the free lands. Ride forth across low hills and keep-shadowed plains, rally every lord still standing, and gather a host mighty enough to seal the Rift — before the violet corruption swallows the Citadel of Dawn.
